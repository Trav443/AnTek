  To liberate
  To hide

  To fight
  Fight for the right to be forgotten
  To be autonomous

  Whatever it may be
  Whenever it may hide

  From the cypherpunk of the past
  To the hacktivists of the present
  We are going to liberate each other
  Whatever it may cost

  So love
  Fight
  Cry
  And smile
  But do not fear

  For you are one of us
  And we are one of you
  We are anarcho-techists
  The saviors of this sad world
  The protectors of freedom and keepers of truth
  The teachers of philosophy
  And protectors of the innocent

  And to those who hold power over the individual
  Fear us”
